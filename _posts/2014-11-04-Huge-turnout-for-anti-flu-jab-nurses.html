--- layout: post title: "Huge turnout for antivaccine nurse march" date: 2014-11-04 17:29:00 categories: namv crazy nurses comments: true ---

"Nurses against Mandatory Vaccines" wowed the world today with a huge turnout of two people to their organised march against... vaccines. This is fifty percent more than the "Billion Toddler March", which consisted of one crazy cat lady.

NAMV have stated that they are thrilled at the success of the march, and if current trends hold up, they will expect four people next year.
