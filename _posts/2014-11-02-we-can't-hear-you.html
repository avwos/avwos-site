--- layout: post title: "We can't hear you" date: 2014-11-02 14:21:00 categories: jekyll update ---

In latest news, anti vaxers claim that the louder they shout - the more real their claims.

This doesn't come as a shock to many people that have been trying to talk to anti vaxers over the years. Mary O'connel of Long Beach, CA was recently admitted to hospital after enduring a five hour online conversation with such a person.

At first it was ok, but then I noticed the amount of capital letters were increasing, until it was all I could see. Eventually, I went blind from all the visual shouting.

The anti vaxer in question was reached out to for comment, but we were not able to make out what he was saying due to all the shouting.
